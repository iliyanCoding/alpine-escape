Ski downhill, dodge the dangers, and rack up the highest score.

Start Playing

How to Play

You're racing down a snowy mountain. The longer you stay alive, the higher your score climbs — but the slope only gets more crowded and more dangerous the further you go.

  • Weave around the trees and tree stumps scattered down the piste.
  • Don't trust the snowmen — every one of them is a wolf in disguise, waiting to pop out and leap at you as you pass.
  • Watch out for lift cards — they'll pelt you with snowballs from the side of the track.
  • Stay on the track — going off-piste slows you down.
  • You can take two hits, but the third crash ends your run. Save your score at the end if it's a personal best.

Cheats

Want to see how far the mountain can really go? There's a secret key that makes you untouchable.

I
Toggle invincibility. Trees, stumps, wolves, and snowballs can't hurt you, and your skier gains a golden glow while it's active. Press I again to turn it off.
